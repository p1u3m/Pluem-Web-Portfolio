Parody Painting
The atmosphere of art creation and presentation is filled with tension and intensity of the content. Artists try to break the tense atmosphere by using a parody of "The girl with pearl earring" instead of a woman, it is a sad-looking frog wearing a pearl earring. Additionally, there is a label mentioning a Thai fried rice recipe that is not related to the artwork at all.
