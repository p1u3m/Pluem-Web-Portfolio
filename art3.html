Nothing happened

3d Modeling & Experimental
The political situation in China is still unstable, combined with the influence of the Chinese meme trend that has become viral on the internet. Studies and observations are being conducted to explore the reactions of people in the meme community towards the 3D modeling artwork created by the artist, which reference Man vs. tank in Tiananmen square. The artworks replace the tanks with cats from the animation "Tom and Jerry", and the man standing in front of the tank is transformed into SpongeBob SquarePants. The presentation of these images represents a form of non-sensical humor and replaces the contradiction of the political conflict with memes.
