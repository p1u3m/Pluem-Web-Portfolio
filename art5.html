Reflection of illusion

Video Art
Thai political history is full of insincerity and deception. The constitution, which was once considered a sacred document, was actually based on lies and the aftermath of violence. This piece of art was created to commemorate and mourn the events of October 14th, 1973, when a violent crackdown by the government, led by Prime Minister Thanom Kittikachorn, occurred on protesters near the Grand Palace and Ratchadamnoen Avenue, resulting in the deaths of tens of people, injuries of over 800 people, and many more missing.
